## Operators

Introduction to operators

---

### Arithmetic Operators
- `+`
- `-`
- `/`
- `*`
- `%`

>[!WARNING]
>The modulus operator can only be used with integers

### Logical Operators
- `!`
- `>, >=, <, <=`
- `==, !=`
- `&&`
- `||`

>[!INFO]-
>These are written in order of precedence
